Color

All chrome colors via CSS custom properties. Authored in OKLCH — neutrals tinted toward hue 278 (indigo) for subconscious cohesion. Semantic only: no raw hex in components.

Note: --fg-subtle (placeholder) does not meet AA at normal text sizes by design — it signals non-interactive state. The live contrast checker in the Style panel catches any user-customized card colors that drift below AA.

Usage note

60-30-10 distribution. Background and surface tokens cover 60% of screen area (bg, surface, surface-2). Neutral foreground and border tokens cover 30% (fg, fg-muted, border, border-strong). Accent covers 10% — focus rings, selected states, the brand logo mark, and the Publish button. Restraint here is load-bearing: the card in the playground must always be the most colorful thing on screen.

Tinted neutrals. All neutral tokens carry ~0.006 chroma toward hue 278 (indigo) in OKLCH. The tint is invisible in isolation but creates subconscious cohesion with the accent — the chrome feels like it belongs to the same family without competing.
